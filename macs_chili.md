# Mac's Chili

## Ingredients

- 1 lb. Ground beef
- 1 can of black beans
- 1 can of kidney beans
- 1 Onion
- 1 Bell Pepper
- 1 tbsp. Smoked Paprika
- 1 tbsp. Chili powder
- 1/3 cup Chilies, diced

## Preparation

1. Dice the onions, pepper.
2. Cook the ground beef, mix with the onions and peppers.
3. Put the beef-onion-pepper mixture into a crock pot. Add the spices. Add 1 cup of water.
4. Cook in the crock pot for 6 hours on low.
